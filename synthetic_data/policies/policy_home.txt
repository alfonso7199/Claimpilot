HOME INSURANCE POLICY - GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Fictitious Insurer Inc. (synthetic document for demo)
Line: HOME. Policy prefix: HOME-2026-xxxxx

Art. 1. Purpose.
Covers material damage to the insured home and its contents, as well as the civil
liability of the insured in the domestic sphere.

Art. 2. Included coverages.
2.1 Water damage.
2.2 Fire, explosion and lightning strike.
2.3 Theft and robbery of the contents.
2.4 Family civil liability.
2.5 Breakage of glass and sanitary fittings.

Art. 3. Water damage.
3.1 Material damage caused by accidental leaks, bursts or overflows of the home's
water pipes is covered.
3.2 Repair of floors, walls and furniture damaged by the water is included.
3.3 Indemnity limit for water damage: EUR 15,000 per claim.
3.4 Deductible applicable to water damage: EUR 150 per claim.
3.5 Locating and repairing the fault that caused the leak (e.g. replacing a hose)
is covered up to EUR 500.

Art. 4. Obligations of the insured.
4.1 Take the necessary measures to mitigate the consequences of the claim (e.g.
shutting off the main stopcock).
4.2 Keep the remains and parts that caused the damage when possible, available to
the adjuster.

Art. 5. Loss adjusting.
5.1 Water damage with an estimated cost above EUR 2,000 will require an adjustment
prior to payment.

Art. 6. Exclusions.
6.1 Damage due to lack of maintenance or pre-existing dampness.
6.2 Damage arising from works or renovations not reported.
6.3 Claims declared with false data or fraudulently.
