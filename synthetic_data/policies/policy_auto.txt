AUTO INSURANCE POLICY - GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Fictitious Insurer Inc. (synthetic document for demo)
Line: AUTO. Policy prefix: AUTO-2026-xxxxx

Art. 1. Purpose.
This policy covers the material damage suffered by the insured vehicle and the
civil liability towards third parties arising from its circulation.

Art. 2. Included coverages.
2.1 Mandatory and voluntary civil liability.
2.2 Own damage from collision, impact or rollover.
2.3 Glass breakage.
2.4 Theft and larceny of the vehicle (see Art. 5, specific conditions).
2.5 Fire.

Art. 3. Collision damage.
3.1 Material damage to the insured vehicle caused by collision with another
vehicle or object is covered, whether in circulation or parked.
3.2 Indemnity limit per own-damage claim: EUR 30,000.
3.3 Deductible applicable to own collision damage: EUR 300 per claim.
3.4 When there is an identified liable third party, the deductible may be
reimbursed after the claim against the liable party.

Art. 4. Loss adjusting.
4.1 Claims with an estimated repair cost above EUR 2,000 will require the
intervention of an adjuster before payment.
4.2 Claims below EUR 2,000 with clear liability may be processed via the fast
track (direct payment) without an on-site adjustment.

Art. 5. Vehicle theft.
5.1 Theft coverage requires that a report be filed with the police authorities
within a maximum of 48 hours from when the event was detected.
5.2 The insured must hand over all sets of keys to the vehicle.
5.3 Payment is excluded if no official police report is provided or if there are
indications of a staged claim.
5.4 The amount will be paid, unless proven otherwise, to the bank account on file
in the policy.

Art. 6. General exclusions.
6.1 Damage caused intentionally by the insured.
6.2 Claims declared with deliberately false or incomplete data.
6.3 Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
